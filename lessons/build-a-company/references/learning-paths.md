# Learning paths

Pre-built 5-item paths organized by goal. Use these as templates — mix and match based on the user's specific needs.

## Communication and influence

For users who want to level up how they communicate, get buy-in, and collaborate effectively.

1. `communication-mistakes.md` — Start by understanding common pitfalls
2. `getting-advice.md` — Learn how to seek and use input from others
3. `collaboration.md` — Rethink how you work with others
4. `shipping.md` — Communication's ultimate output: getting things shipped
5. `50-product-learnings.md` — Broader context on how communication fits into product work

## Product craft

For users who want to sharpen their product instincts and ship better work.

1. `validating-ideas.md` — Start with knowing what to build
2. `shipping.md` — Then learn how to actually get it out the door
3. `50-product-learnings.md` — Absorb a dense set of hard-won lessons
4. `vibe-designing.md` — Use new tools to move faster on design
5. `pricing.md` — Understand the commercial side of what you build

## AI and the future

For users curious about how AI is changing the way products get built.

1. `engineeringification.md` — The big picture: how roles are shifting
2. `ai-coding-mistakes.md` — Practical mistakes to avoid with AI coding
3. `vibe-designing.md` — AI applied to design and product work
4. `product-engineer.md` — How the product engineer role fits in this new world
5. `50-product-learnings.md` — Timeless lessons that still apply regardless of tooling

## Startup fundamentals

For users building something from scratch who need to understand the full picture.

1. `pm-role.md` — Understand the product function, even if you don't have a PM
2. `pricing.md` — Get the commercial basics right early
3. `validating-ideas.md` — Don't build the wrong thing
4. `collaboration.md` — Set up your team to move fast
5. `shipping.md` — Make shipping the default, not the exception

## Career growth

For users thinking about their own trajectory and how to become more impactful.

1. `product-engineer.md` — Understand where the industry is heading
2. `finding-a-job.md` — Whether you're hiring or job-hunting
3. `getting-advice.md` — Build a support system for your growth
4. `engineeringification.md` — Prepare for how your role will evolve
5. `communication-mistakes.md` — The soft skill that compounds the most
